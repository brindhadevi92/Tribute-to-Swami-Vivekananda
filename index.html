Swami Vivekananda

  • 1863 - Born on January 12, 1863 in Calcutta.
  • 1869 - At the age of 6, goes to pathshala.
  • 1879 - Goes to the Presidency College.
  • 1881 - Meets Sri Ramakrishna in the General Assembly Insitution.
  • 1893 - Arrives in Chicago to represent Hinduism and deliver his message to the other cultures.
  • 1897 - Creates the Ramakrishna Mission, an organization that focuses on different social services.
  • 1899 - Creates Belur Math, an organization that focused on monastic life of monks.
  • 1902 - Dies at the age of just 39.
